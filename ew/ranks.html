Staff ranks:

Owner

Handles the finances of the server. The owner has the final say in every major network decision.

Admin

The servers are run by Administrators, who are responsible for making server-wide decisions.

Developper

Developpers handle everything on the server that contains a line of code. They program new game features such as abilities, commands, etc...

Moderator

Responsible for keeping the day-to-day Empire War experience as enjoyable as possible. They make sure players follow our rules and work behind the scenes on balancing classes and preparing server updates.

Jr. Moderator

Similarly to Moderators, Jr. Moderstors are usually players who have recently been picked to join the staff team and are assisting the rest of the moderation team with their everyday tasks while also learning from them.

Builder

Responsible for all changes where building is required wether it be map updates or lobbies. The maps themselves are created by EpicQuestz, a build-team and our build server, but the Empire War Builders are able to make quick adjustments and changes directly on the PvP server.
